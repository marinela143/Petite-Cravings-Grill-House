About Us

We are the proud owner and manager of PETITE CRAVING'S GRILL HOUSE, our local samgyupsal haven. Growing up, food has always been our love language—it’s how I bond with our family and friends, and how we create memories. When we discovered Korean BBQ, we instantly fell in love with its unique mix of flavor, fun, and togetherness. That experience sparked a dream: to bring the same joy and connection to our community.

Starting this business wasn’t easy for us. We had a vision to offer affordable, quality samgyupsal in a space where everyone—from students and families to coworkers and friends—could come, relax, and enjoy great food without worrying about breaking the bank. It took months of planning, tasting (lots of tasting!), and designing an atmosphere that felt warm and inviting.
